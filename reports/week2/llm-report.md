# LLM Usage Report

## AI/LLM Tools Used

During Assignment 2 preparation, the team used the following AI/LLM tool:

* ChatGPT

---

## Areas of AI/LLM Usage

AI assistance was used in the following areas:

### Documentation and Writing

* improving grammar and readability of documents;
* structuring written reports;
* refining user stories;
* formatting Markdown documentation;
* preparing meeting summaries and transcripts.

### Research and Idea Generation

* brainstorming UX improvements;
* generating questionnaire improvement ideas;
* discussing possible fragrance recommendation approaches;
* refining wording and interface concepts.

### Transcription and Organization

* restructuring customer meeting notes into a formal transcript;
* summarizing customer feedback;
* organizing assignment documentation.

### Coding and Technical Assistance

* discussing implementation approaches;
* clarifying technical concepts;
* assisting with API documentation formatting.

---

## Human Oversight

All AI-generated content was reviewed, edited, and validated by team members before inclusion in the final submission.

The team remained fully responsible for:

* technical implementation;
* design decisions;
* questionnaire logic;
* UX decisions;
* final written content;
* overall project direction.

---

## Product-Level AI Usage

At the current MVP stage, no AI or LLM functionality is integrated directly into the product itself.

The recommendation system currently relies on planned questionnaire-based logic rather than AI-generated recommendations.

---

## Conclusion

AI/LLM tools were used as supportive assistants during Assignment 2 preparation, documentation, brainstorming, and organizational tasks.

Final decisions and implementation choices were made by the team members.
