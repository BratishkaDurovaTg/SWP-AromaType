# Customer Meeting Summary

## Date

June 12, 2026

## Participants

* Customer
* Product Team

## Purpose

Review of Assignment 2 deliverables, including user stories, Figma prototype, questionnaire methodology, and Telegram Mini App MVP v0.

---

## Artifacts Presented

* User Stories
* Figma Interactive Prototype
* Questionnaire Draft
* Telegram Mini App MVP v0

---

## Main Discussion Points

### User Stories

The customer reviewed the provided user stories and confirmed that they were acceptable. However, the customer requested more detailed UX flows and screen transition descriptions.

### Design and Color Palette

The customer stated that the current design feels outdated and visually inconsistent. The existing green and brown palette was considered acceptable conceptually, but the customer recommended using deeper and more natural colors such as olive, beige, and wood-inspired tones.

The customer also requested multiple alternative color palettes for approval.

### Typography

The customer identified inconsistent typography usage across the prototype. Multiple font families were used within the same frame, which was considered inappropriate.

The customer recommended:

* using a consistent font family across the application;
* avoiding decorative or calligraphic fonts;
* using modern rounded minimalist fonts such as Montserrat.

### UX and Navigation

The customer emphasized the importance of detailed UX flows and button-level interaction descriptions.

The current user stories were considered too general.

### Questionnaire Methodology

The customer requested a more research-based approach for questionnaire design. The current questionnaire was created independently by the team without external sources.

The customer recommended using:

* scientific publications;
* fragrance recommendation research;
* industry-related articles.

### Telegram Mini App

The customer pointed out that the Mini App currently redirects users to a browser, which is not acceptable.

The application should remain fully inside Telegram during usage.

---

## Decisions

* The team will redesign the color palette.
* Typography will be standardized.
* The questionnaire methodology will be improved using external research.
* UX flows and user stories will be expanded in more detail.
* Telegram Mini App integration behavior will be corrected.

---

## Action Items

1. Prepare multiple color palette options.
2. Improve typography consistency.
3. Expand UX flow documentation.
4. Revise questionnaire questions using research-based sources.
5. Fix Telegram Mini App browser redirection behavior.

---

## Outcome

The customer approved the general project direction and user stories but requested improvements in design consistency, UX detailing, questionnaire methodology, and Telegram Mini App integration before the next review.
