# Sprint Retrospective

## What Went Well

1. We successfully migrated all active user stories into GitHub Issues and created a structured Product Backlog with supporting technical PBIs.

2. We refined the backlog by adding priorities, acceptance criteria, and traceability between requirements and issues.

3. We established the Sprint workflow by creating Product and Sprint Backlogs, defining the Definition of Done, and preparing MVP v1 planning artifacts.

## What Did Not Go Well

1. Story point estimation required additional refinement.

2. Some features planned for future iterations still need further clarification before implementation.

3. MVP scope and Sprint planning decisions are still evolving as the backlog becomes more detailed.

## Action Points

1. Finalize backlog estimation and Sprint planning earlier in the next Sprint.

2. Continue implementing MVP v1 functionality and preparing remaining backlog items for future Sprints.
